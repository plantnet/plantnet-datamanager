 - Datamanager

Pl@ntNet-Datamanager is a distributed data management system dedicated to botany. With this software, you can :

  • Create databases with your own data organisation.
  • Send / Retrieve data to / from other Pl@ntNet-Datamanager instances.
  • Manage your data locally on your computer. Pl@ntNet-Datamanager is decentralized. The software can be installed on servers but also on personnal computers.
